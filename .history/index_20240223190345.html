What is Asia Kakehashi Project+?

The Asia KAKEHASHI Project+ is a cultural exchange initiative funded by MEXT Japan, offering full scholarships for high school students from Asia, North America, and Europe to study in Japan for a semester or academic year. The program aims to strengthen Japan-Asia relations by fostering cross-cultural understanding and exposing students to diverse perspectives, cultures, and industries.
